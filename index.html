How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

No folders. No tags. No organizing. Just save and forget — Cache handles the rest.

Save anything

One click or ⌘+Shift+S from any website. Articles, threads, PDFs, selections, videos.

Automatic summarization, key-point extraction, and semantic embedding. Happens in seconds, silently.
